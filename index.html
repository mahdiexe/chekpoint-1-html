What is Gomycode

Based in Sfax, Tunisia, GOMYCODE is a school that offers instructor-led training programs to help people develop digital skills for careers in fields like cybersecurity, UX design, web development, graphic design, data science, and digital marketing. Their programs are designed to be accessible to beginners with no prior technical experience, and they also offer bootcamps for those seeking more intensive training. They even have certifications from companies like Adobe, Microsoft, and Meta

About contacting me

There are a few ways to contact someone on their website. The most convenient method is usually checking for a dedicated "Contact" section. This section often features a contact form where you can fill in your details and message, and it will be sent directly to the website owner's email. Alternatively, some websites might display the owner's email address directly on the "Contact" page or footer. In other cases, you might find social media links, allowing you to connect with them on platforms like LinkedIn or Twitter. If none of these options are available, searching for a "mailto:" link with the owner's email address might be a last resort, although it's less common nowadays.

About login

Secure login is the gateway to your online accounts. It typically involves entering your email address, which acts as a unique identifier, and a password, a secret code chosen by you. The website or application verifies your credentials by checking if the entered email matches an existing account and if the password corresponds to that account. This two-step process helps ensure only authorized users can access your information.
